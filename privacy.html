Privacy Policy

Introduction

At BreatheClo, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Customer-provided: Name, email, shipping/billing addresses, phone number, payment details, account credentials.
  • Automatically collected: IP address, device info, browsing data via cookies and tracking tools.
  • Third-party: Data from social logins and payment processors.

2. How We Use Information

  • To process orders and prevent fraud
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To personalize your shopping experience and recommend products
  • To analyze site usage and improve performance
  • To inform you about promotions (opt-in only)
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality, performance, and marketing (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel). You may manage cookie preferences at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with trusted third parties (payment gateways, shipping services, marketing tools, legal authorities when required). We do not sell personal data.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to external sites; we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard protections (SSL, encryption, access controls). No method is infallible.

7. Your Rights

  • Access or update your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of promotional messages
  • Withdraw consent for tracking analytics
  • Lodge complaints with your local data protection authority

8. Children’s Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy—changes will be posted with an updated date.
